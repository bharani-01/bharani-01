// Who I Am

Beyond the Code

I'm a B.Tech Cyber Security & IoT student at SRET Chennai who genuinely believes that the most exciting engineering happens at the intersection of hardware and intelligence. I didn't start in AI or embedded — I started by breaking things apart, figuring out how they worked, and putting them back together better.

That curiosity led me from basic web projects to building autonomous rovers with real-time telemetry, training custom YOLO models for campus surveillance, and writing production REST APIs in raw C++17. Every project in this portfolio started with a real problem I wanted to solve — not a tutorial I wanted to follow.

When I'm not coding, you'll find me experimenting with ESP32-CAMs, reading about edge AI deployment, or debugging I2C buses at 2 AM because the MPU-6050 decided to stop responding.
